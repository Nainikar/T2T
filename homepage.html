What can we recycle?



Rewards

Reusable water bottles are eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastic. By using a durable stainless steel or glass bottle, an individual can prevent the disposal of approximately 1,460 plastic bottles annually. Making the switch reduces environmental impact, conserves resources, and supports a sustainable, plastic-free future for our planet.

Reusable shopping bags are eco-friendly alternatives, reducing plastic pollution. A single reusable bag can replace hundreds of disposable ones, preventing tons of plastic waste annually. With an average lifespan of over 700 uses, these bags significantly cut down environmental impact, conserving resources and promoting sustainable consumer choices.

Reusable coffee cups are eco-conscious alternatives, significantly reducing single-use cup waste. Americans alone discard 25 billion coffee cups annually. Opting for reusable cups prevents this environmental toll, with each cup saving thousands of disposables. Made from materials like bamboo or stainless steel, they promote sustainability, reducing plastic pollution and carbon footprint.

Bamboo toothbrushes are sustainable alternatives to traditional plastic ones, featuring biodegradable bamboo handles. With over 3.5 billion toothbrushes discarded annually, bamboo toothbrushes combat plastic pollution. Their handles decompose in months, reducing environmental impact. Make a simple switch — one bamboo toothbrush saves multiple plastic ones from entering landfills, supporting a greener planet.

Recycled stationery minimizes environmental impact, using materials crafted from post-consumer or post-industrial waste. Each ton of recycled paper saves approximately 17 trees, conserves 7,000 gallons of water, and reduces energy consumption significantly. Opting for recycled stationery not only supports sustainability but also promotes a circular economy, closing the loop on resource use.

Eco-friendly cleaning products harness the power of nature to enhance cleanliness while minimizing environmental impact. Using plant-based ingredients, these products reduce water pollution and promote sustainability. With biodegradable formulations, they contribute to a significant reduction in plastic waste. Choose green cleaning for a healthier home and planet.

Biodegradable cutlery offers a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic utensils, addressing the global plastic pollution crisis. Crafted from plant-based materials like cornstarch, these utensils decompose naturally, reducing environmental impact. With over 8 million tons of plastic entering oceans annually, making the switch to biodegradable cutlery significantly minimizes plastic waste, supporting a cleaner, healthier planet.

Transform your space sustainably with recycled home decor. Each upcycled item breathes new life into materials that might otherwise end up in landfills. By choosing recycled decor, you contribute to reducing waste; for instance, using reclaimed wood saves over 1,200 pounds of carbon emissions per square foot. Make a stylish, eco-conscious statement.

Planting saplings is a potent tool for environmental restoration. A single tree can absorb up to 48 pounds of carbon dioxide annually, mitigating climate change. Moreover, trees provide oxygen, support biodiversity, and combat soil erosion. Join the green revolution – each sapling planted propels us toward a healthier, more sustainable future.
