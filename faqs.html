FAQs

1. How does the app work?

Our app seamlessly integrates recycling, rewards, and waste collection. Simply sign up, schedule a collection, and earn eco-friendly rewards for your recyclables.

2. How do I book an appointment for waste collection?

Booking is easy! Navigate to the "Book an appointment" section, choose a convenient date and time, and confirm your appointment. Our efficient system ensures a hassle-free process for waste pickup.

3. What kind of recyclable waste can I give for collection?

We accept various recyclables, including paper, plastic, glass, and metal. Check our home page for a comprehensive list of accepted materials.

4. Is there a minimum or maximum amount of recyclable waste required for collection?

No minimum, and we happily accept any amount of recyclables. Whether it's a small batch or a larger collection, we appreciate every effort toward sustainability.

5. How much money or rewards can I earn for my recyclable waste?

Your eco-friendly actions earn you rewards! The value depends on the type and quantity of recyclables. Explore the "Rewards" section for exciting incentives.

6. Can I choose the date and time for the collection appointment?

Absolutely! Our app allows you to select a date and time that suits your schedule, ensuring a convenient and flexible waste collection experience.

7. Is there a fee or charge for using the app or the waste collection service?

No fees! Our app and waste collection service are free to use. We believe in making sustainable practices accessible to all.

8. How do I know if my recyclable waste is eligible for collection?

Refer to our "recyclable" section in home page to determine the eligibility of your recyclables.

9. What happens to the recyclable waste after it is collected?

Collected waste undergoes eco-friendly processing methods, contributing to recycling efforts. We prioritize sustainable disposal practices to minimize environmental impact.

10. Can I track the status of my appointment or collection?

Yes! The app provides real-time updates on your appointment status and the progress of your waste collection, ensuring transparency and peace of mind.

11. How long does it take for the collector to arrive after booking an appointment?

Typically, our collectors aim to arrive promptly within the chosen time slot. Delays are minimal, ensuring a timely and efficient waste collection process.

12. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Certainly! The app allows you to manage your appointments. Cancel or reschedule with ease, ensuring flexibility to accommodate your changing schedule.

13. Is there a limit on the number of appointments I can book?

No limits! Feel free to book as many appointments as needed. We're here to support your commitment to sustainable living.

14. Is the service available in all areas?

We strive to expand our service coverage continuously. Check the app for the most up-to-date information on available service areas.

15. How can I provide feedback or report any issues with the app or the waste collection service?

Your feedback is valuable! Visit the "Contact Us" section in the app to share your thoughts, report issues, or inquire about any concerns. We're dedicated to enhancing your experience.
